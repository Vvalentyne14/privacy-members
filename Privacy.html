Privacy Policy

Privacy and Data Protection

Effective Date: October 16, 2024

1. Introduction

At Mavride, your privacy is our priority. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the personal information of our Members when using our app for transportation services.

2. Information we collect

We collect the following information from Members:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, physical address, medical details (e.g., wheelchair accessibility needs), and payment information (if applicable).
  • Non-Personal Information: Device information, location data during trips, and app usage data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and provide transportation services.
  • To communicate trip details and updates.
  • To ensure transportation meets your specific medical or accessibility needs.
  • To process any required payments.

4. Sharing Your Information

Your data may be shared with:

  • Drivers: To ensure they have the necessary details for your trip.
  • Providers: For scheduling and coordinating transportation.
  • Brokers: To manage trip payments or other approvals (if applicable).

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as necessary to provide transportation services or comply with legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and update your data.
  • Request deletion of your information.
  • Restrict or object to certain types of processing.
